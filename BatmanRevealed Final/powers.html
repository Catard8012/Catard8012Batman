Batman's Powers

Batman's powers primarily reside in his advanced physical abilities and extensive technological resources. He is a master of martial arts, skilled in various forms of combat and weaponry. He possesses peak human strength, agility, endurance, and intelligence. Batman also relies on his gadgets and gear.

Batman's costume is composed of Kevlar and a small percentage of titanium; it is bulletproof and resistant to most forms of attack (explosions, blunt trauma, falls, etc.)

Small bat-shaped throwing weapons. Originally based on Aboriginal Australian boomerangs, more recent years have depicted them as being similar to shuriken.

Small miniature pellets containing chemical components that, when mixed, release airborne agents.

A modified car used for both high-speed pursuits and general travel. Batman often uses the Batmobile to patrol the streets of Gotham or as his general response to situations.

Batman is a skilled fighter and has been trained in multiple martial arts disciplines, including karate, judo, and boxing. He has also been trained in stealth and is skilled in using various weapons, such as batarangs and grappling hooks. His fighting style often involves using strategy and gadgets to gain the upper hand on his opponents, while also relying on his impressive physical strength and agility.
